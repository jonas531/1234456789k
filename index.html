The outstanding student in 2nd Macao Cultural Heritage Little Docent Training Program 2017

Joining a young docent program for Zheng's Family Mansion was a great opportunity for me as I am quite interested in Macau history and East- West mix culture. The mansion is a well-known historical site in Macau and is one of the few remaining combination of Chinese and Western elements style mansions in this region.

As a docent for the mansion, I have the opportunity to share the history and cultural significance of the site with visitors from around the world. This was a great way to develop my public speaking skills and gain experience in communicating with people from different cultures and backgrounds.

The 12th Macao Geography Olympiad Silver prizes April 9, 2023

Of all the achievements that I have accomplished, the most impressive one must be the Twelfth Macao Geography Olympiad. This event changed my thinking in several ways, as I learned a lot from it.

During my preparation for the competition, I dedicated a lot of time to it. Since we received the invitation to join the match in February, we only had less than two months to prepare for it. To increase our chances of winning, we needed to have classes twice a week after school. However, I felt that this was not enough to win, so I studied geography at home every day of the week. I even asked my friends to test me on various topics to ensure that I had a deep understanding of the concepts. When I found out that I had won the silver prize in the competition, I was relieved. Finally, all my hard work paid off after days of intense preparation. I had achieved the greatest result in the team, which was also my first time participating in the competition.

Moreover, it felt like the first time I worked hard to achieve something. My teacher always said that I was the hope of the team. At first, I did not understand what that meant, but now I realize that she was trying to emphasize that I was willing to put more effort into something I enjoyed than others in the team. I felt a sense of excitement and motivation to discover new things that I have never felt before.

In conclusion, the Twelfth Macao Geography Olympiad was a turning point in my life that changed my aims in the future. Although I used to love money and wanted to become an economist, I now yearn for a free life traveling all around the world rather than being trapped in the concrete jungle. I realized that life is short, and there are so many adventures waiting for me out there.
